About This Movement

#BringBackOurGirls

What Happened?

Over 200 Nigerian girls have been abducted by a terrorist group called Boko Haram, who target Christian and Muslims in northern Nigeria. Their name literally means "Western Education is sinful" in the local Hausa language. One thing they are definitely against is the education of women. In a recent video released by Abubakar Shekau, the leader of Boko Haram threatened that the girls in his custody will be sold in the market.

What You Can Do?

  • Organize a campaign about it in your location.
